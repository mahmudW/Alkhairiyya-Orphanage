edit_square PROBLEM STATEMENT

"Alhumdulillah! Indeed, all praise is due to Allah. We praise Him and seek His Help and forgiveness. We seek refuge in Allah from our souls' evils and our wrong doings. He whom Allah guides, no one can misguide; and he whom He misguides, no one can guide."

The recent increase in socio-economic challenges and ever increase in conflicts and other calamities has of recent given rise to unproportionally increase in number of orphans especially in Northern Nigeria. This has adverse effect to the orphans in specifically attaining the objective of the sustainable Development Goal 4, which is to ensure Quality Education. It is about lifelong learning, to which it is understood that Early stimulation increases duration of schooling, school performance and adult income.

The absence of parental care especially at early stage of life deprives orphans sound emotional, physical, social and intellectual development. These are key factors that indicate whether a child will grow to become responsible and happy adult.

The absence of positive parenting can also endanger peace at home and in communities and also the safeguard of the future of societies.

All these have direct impact on the welfare, peace and stability of every society. Therefore, without corresponding increase in support for the orphans, safeguard of the society will continue to be weakened.

Hence, the reason Allah (SWT) placed the care of orphans on every individual and by extension the whole society.

“Concerning the world and the Hereafter. They ask you about the orphans. Say, “Setting right (their affairs) for them (is) best. And if you associate with them then they (are) your brothers. And Allah knows the corrupter from the amender. And if (had) willed Allah surely He (could have) put you in difficulties. Indeed, Allah (is) All-Mighty, All-Wise.” (Al-Baqarah 2:220)

At public level, rulers have been directed to make sure the orphans are being given their share of charity and zakat. As far as Islam is concerned orphans must be adequately taken care of.

A glimmer of hope for the orphans of Zangon Aya and its environment is the formation of this foundation with its objectives to support their educational and skills development needs. Thus, empowering the orphans to grab the boundless chances and opportunities to tackle the limitless challenges of life.

This would be in the provision of educational and vocational life opportunities for the orphans from Lower Basic up to the University level inshaa Allah. This, being the modest contribution of the foundation to the realization of the rights of orphans generally as instructed in the holy Quran, Chapter 2 Verse 215: `They ask you, (O Muhammad), what they should spend (in charity). Say, Whatever you spend of good is (to be) for parents and relatives and orphans and the needy and the traveler. And whatever you do of good -indeed, Allah is Knowing of it”

Recent orphans registration in Zangon Aya town alone has revealed over 150 orphans of ages between 3-6 years from a population of about 15000 inhabitants. Most of the orphans have no access to formal education and may likely remain unattended to in the absence of this modest support. Thus, this foundation attempts to solve a very strong and critical social problem in the town and its environment.

Therefore, the need for support from governments, organizations, corporate bodies and well-meaning and highly spirited individuals in becoming partners to achieve the foundation’s objectives is highly essential and would be appreciated.

We beseech Allahs’ blessing to purify our intentions and guide us in all our future endevors.
