Alkhairiyya Orphans Education Foundation

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision
The organization is initiated by some highly spirited individuals to answer the various calls by our creator - Allah (SWT) in the holy Quran Who placed the care of orpahns on every individual and by extension the whole society as highlighted earlier on.

It is also expected that other highly spirited individuals and organizatios will join hands together here as partners in the promotion of this noble objectives.

Collectively, our efforts is to be dedicated to and on behalf of our living and departed parents as Sadaqat-aljariah for both of us.
We pray to Allah (SWT) our creator to forgive all our shortcomings, accept the modest efforts of all and reward us abundantly.
Muasassatu Riayatu Liaunil Aytam. Organization founded for voluntary assistance to cater for all orphans irrespective of religion, tribe, color or nationality.
Safeguarding the future of orphans and vulnerable young ones.

edit_square AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

  • Provision of standard educational and skills training facilities, that will see the orphans acquiring formal and Islamic knowledge from lower basic up to secondary levels in both systems.
  • Uplifting the living standard of orphans and vulnerable children.
  • Fulfilling their inherent potential which consist of unique abilities, identities, interest and social capabilities, all of which we need to discover, protect and develop by involving them in a continuous program which will address in a holistic fashion.
  • To contribute towards a healthy community through family life, parenting awareness development of a network of caregivers and community leadership mobilization on behalf of children.

edit_square DEVELOPMENTS PLANS AND GOALS

The objectives of the foundation is to provide standard world class education (formal and Islamiyya) from primary school up to university level Inshaa Allah.
Therefore, the foundation intends to initiate a 20 years development plan divided in to short and long term to lay a solid foundation for the achievement of its overall objectives.

  • Short Term Development

The 10 years short term development plan will concentrate on the development of basic level and secondary level educational facilities. This will see to the establishment of organized educational institution up to secondary school level and award of secondary school certificates.

  • Long Term Development

The long term development plan will take care of long term initiatives and goals that will see to the consolidation of the short term achievements as well as projecting it to higher level inshaa Allah.
